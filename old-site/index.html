About us

Founded in 2012 ALTAMKEEN is based in Sharjah - Ind15, in the heart of natural stone and marble market.

We are a dynamic and consolidated company with a regional focus, our activity ranges from the purchase of marble, granite, travertine and natural stone in all forms, to the transformation into the realization of complete and customized projects.

The company philosophy is to provide the highest quality and best customer service by ensuring a careful control on all production levels, and to our many years of experience and passion.

Our aim is to serve the most demanding customers, as well as architects and designers, through the highest quality of services and the suitable solutions for each different need.
